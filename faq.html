Frequently Asked Questions

What is open source?

"Open source" is a way for us to share what we've done with the world.

Why are you releasing all your hard work for free?

We've benefited a lot from all the open source we've used in the past and wanted to pay it forward.

By building a community that others can join helps all of us rise with the tide.

How do to make money?
We don't... right now. This project isn't yet about making money, but if you need help with designs, or renting a boat, let us know!
